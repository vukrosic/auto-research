- **embed bottleneck peaks at 320.** 128→192→256→288→**320**→384↓. monotonic improvement reverses at 384 — the 1024→384→384 projection becomes near-identity and wastes params. didn't expect a hard peak this sharp.
- **surprise second: logit softcap 30→15.** tightening the output cap gave -0.54%. nobody's testing this knob. now it's on the list.
- **combos fail at 500 steps.** bn320+depth, bn320+attnres_vr — both worse than bn320 alone. each change needs its own training budget. stack later at 4k steps.
- **weight sharing: dead at this scale.** 5 unique blocks × 2 cycles = 10 effective layers, but +1.3% worse. shared weights don't specialize fast enough in 500 steps.
- **value_residual attention still works alone** (-0.32%), just doesn't compound yet.

bn320 and logit_cap15 survive. both scale to 4k steps next. weight sharing, cumsum, weighted_vector — eliminated.
